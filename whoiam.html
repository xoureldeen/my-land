NOURELDEEN ELSAYED HUSSAIN AWAD

Born in **2006** in **Benghazi, Libya**, **Noureldeen Elsayed Hussain Awad** is an Egyptian artist with Moroccan roots, operating under the alias **DABSH**.

More than just a music producer, DABSH is a multifaceted artist—a **DJ, Sound Engineer, Sound Designer, Composer, and Art Director**. His work is driven by a singular vision for a new, unapologetic art form. Currently based in **Cairo (Maadi)** after starting his journey in Ismailia, he crafts music that is a direct manifestation of his artistic heart.

His signature style is a revolutionary fusion of **Egyptian Chaabi, Algerian Raï, and Oriental music** with the raw energy of **electronic dance genres** like **Jersey Club, Techno, and House**. He believes the sounds of the streets deserve to be heard by everyone, creating original, boundary-pushing folklore that intentionally deviates from commercial norms.

*— Initial production work crafted using FL Studio.*
